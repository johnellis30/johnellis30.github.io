KAWAI VIOLIN

"Happiness is a thing to be practiced, like the violin"

John Lubbock

Lisa Kawai

A talented Suzuki Method trained violinist with decades of experience. As a former member of the Chiba Symphony Orchestra, Waseda Orchestra, and DC Chamber Music Orchestra, she has a proven history of mastering classical music. However, Lisa has also taken a more modern approach to the violin with covers of top popular music through videos and electric violin performances.

Lisa's goal is to share her experience through teaching, collaboration, and performances. Please see the below links for further information.
